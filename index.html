Ballista - A JavaScript library for creating social media buttons

Version 1.0

Examples

ballista.createTwitterShareButton("#ballista_anchor_twitter_share", "Check out my sweet page", true, "brettwgreen", "brettwgreen", "awesome", true);
ballista.createTwitterFollowButton("#ballista_anchor_twitter_follow", "brettwgreen", true, "brettwgreen", "brettwgreen", "awesome", true);
ballista.createTwitterHashtagButton("#ballista_anchor_twitter_hashtag", "omg", "He's at it again", "brettwgreen,twitter", "http://brettwgreen.com", true, true);
ballista.createTwitterMentionButton("#ballista_anchor_twitter_mention", "brettwgreen", "Wazup my man", "twitter", true, true);
ballista.createFacebookShareButton("#ballista_anchor_fb_share", "http://www.google.com");
